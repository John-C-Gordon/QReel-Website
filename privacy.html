Privacy Policy

Last updated 9 June 2026

Plain-language summary. QReel makes QR codes on your device. We keep what we collect to a minimum, never sell your data, and only store your codes if you choose to sync them to an account. Bracketed items below are placeholders for you to confirm before publishing.

This Privacy Policy explains how [Your Company Name] ("QReel", "we", "us") handles information when you use the QReel app for iPhone, iPad and Mac and this website. By using QReel you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. If you create an account to sync your codes, we store your email address and an authentication identifier. Your password is handled by our authentication provider and is never stored by us in plain text.
  • Content you create. The text, URLs, contact details, Wi-Fi credentials and design settings you put into a QR code. If you don't sign in, this stays on your device.
  • Scan analytics. For codes where you enable URL scan tracking, we record scan events — timestamp, approximate region, and device type — so we can show you scan counts and trends. We do not collect the personal identity of the people who scan your codes.
  • Diagnostic & usage data. Basic, aggregated information such as app version, crash reports and feature usage, used to keep the app stable.

How we use your information

  • To generate, style, save and sync your QR codes across your devices.
  • To provide scan-tracking analytics for codes where you turn the feature on.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot and improve the app.
  • To respond to your support requests.

Where your data is stored

When you sync to an account, your codes and account data are stored with our backend and database provider on secure, access-controlled servers. Data is encrypted in transit. We retain your synced data for as long as your account is active.

Scan tracking

Scan tracking is opt-in per code. When enabled, scanning a code routes briefly through our service to count the scan before forwarding to your destination. We aggregate these events into the analytics you see in the app. You can disable tracking or delete a tracked code at any time, which stops new data from being collected.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run QReel (for example, authentication, database hosting and the App Store) and only as needed to provide the service, or when required by law.

Your choices & rights
