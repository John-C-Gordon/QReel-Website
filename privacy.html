Privacy Policy

Last updated 5 July 2026

Plain-language summary. QReel makes QR codes on your device. We keep what we collect to a minimum, never sell your data, and only store your codes if you choose to sync them to an account.

This Privacy Policy explains how QReel ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the QReel app for iPhone, iPad and Mac and this website. By using QReel you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. If you create an account to sync your codes, we store the email address or phone number you use to sign in, along with an authentication identifier. You may also sign in with Apple or Google — in these cases we receive only the identifier and, if provided, the name or email your chosen provider shares with us; we do not see your Apple or Google password. If you sign in with your phone number, we send a one-time passcode by SMS to verify it — we use a third-party messaging provider to deliver it, we use your number only to sign you in (never for marketing), and standard message and data rates may apply. If you set a password, it's handled by our authentication provider and never stored by us in plain text.
  • Content you create. The text, URLs, contact details, Wi-Fi credentials and design settings you put into a QR code, as well as any images or photos you upload as logos or pixel-fill design elements. If you don't sign in, this stays on your device. If you sync to an account, your codes and uploaded images are stored securely and linked to your account.
  • Scan analytics. For codes where you enable URL scan tracking, we record scan events — timestamp, approximate region, and device type — so we can show you scan counts and trends. We do not collect the personal identity of the people who scan your codes.
  • Subscription and purchase data. If you subscribe to QReel Pro, Apple's App Store processes the payment and our subscription provider receives confirmation of your subscription status to unlock pro features. We do not see or store your payment card details.
  • Diagnostic & usage data. Basic, aggregated information such as app version, crash reports and feature usage, used to keep the app stable.

How we use your information

  • To generate, style, save and sync your QR codes across your devices.
  • To provide scan-tracking analytics for codes where you turn the feature on.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot and improve the app.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To verify your identity and sign you in, including sending one-time passcodes to your email or phone.

Where your data is stored

When you sync to an account, your codes and account data are stored with our backend and database provider on secure, access-controlled servers. Data is encrypted in transit. We retain your synced data for as long as your account is active.

Scan tracking

Scan tracking is opt-in per code. When enabled, scanning a code routes briefly through our service to count the scan before forwarding to your destination. We aggregate these events into the analytics you see in the app. You can disable tracking or delete a tracked code at any time, which stops new data from being collected.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run QReel, and only as needed to provide the service or when required by law. Our key providers include: Supabase (authentication, database and file storage); Cloudflare (scan-redirect infrastructure — processes the country, city and device type recorded for scan analytics); RevenueCat (subscription and purchase management); and Apple (App Store payments and, where used, Sign in with Apple). If you use phone sign-in, an SMS provider delivers your one-time passcode.

Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data. You can delete individual codes in the app, or request full account deletion by emailing us. Where laws such as the GDPR or CCPA apply, we honor those rights for residents of the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California and other regions with comparable data-protection laws.

Data retention & deletion

You can delete saved codes at any time from within the app. To delete your entire account and associated data, contact [email protected] and we will process the request within a reasonable period.

Children's privacy

QReel is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and review our practices regularly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "last updated" date above, and where appropriate we will notify you in the app.
